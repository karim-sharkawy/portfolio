# Guest Speaker at West Lafayette Junior/Senior High School

I presented to middle and high school students on the role of mathematics in theoretical and applied contexts, drawing from examples in atmospheric dynamics and operator theory.

## Topics Covered
- How mathematics simplifies complex systems
- Modeling relationships and revealing underlying structure
- The nature of research and personal growth in mathematical understanding
- Nonlinear paths in learning and the value of intellectual struggle

## Presentation Materials
- Slides on the applicability of mathematics
- Examples from atmospheric science and linear algebra

This experience reinforced my commitment to demystifying advanced subjects and encouraging students to embrace the challenges of learning.
