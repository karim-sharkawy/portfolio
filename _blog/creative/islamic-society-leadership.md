# Community Leadership at Islamic Society of Greater Lafayette

From January 2023 through December 2024, I served as a community volunteer and leader with the Islamic Society of Greater Lafayette, guiding and coordinating a diverse group of students and community members.

## Key Responsibilities
- Led a team of 90 students and community members from various cultural backgrounds
- Fostered an inclusive environment rooted in mutual respect and sustainability
- Shaped community initiatives to be responsive to diverse needs and priorities

## Personal Growth
This experience taught me the importance of patience, resilience, and long-term thinking when working toward systemic change. I learned to balance empathy with accountability and empower others to take leadership roles.

## Impact
Through sustained efforts, we saw meaningful increases in female engagement within the community, demonstrating the value of trusting the process in cultural shifts.
