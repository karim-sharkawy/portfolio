# A Mango-Shaped Space — Wendy Mass

A young adult novel about a girl who experiences synesthesia, where numbers and letters have colors. The story explores themes of difference, self-acceptance, and the beauty of unique perceptions.

## Themes
- Neurodiversity and synesthesia
- Friendship and family
- Self-discovery

## Why I Read It
This book offered a creative perspective on perception and cognition, which I found fascinating in relation to my work in data visualization and pattern recognition.
