# Earth Day Educational Outreach

As part of Purdue University's Earth, Atmospheric, and Planetary Sciences outreach, I helped design and deliver interactive programming for elementary school students focused on Earth Day.

## Activities
- Used the concept of barcodes to explain how scientists detect and analyze exoplanet atmospheres
- Taught children about energy conservation and reducing household carbon footprints
- Created engaging, understandable content about environmental responsibility

## Approach
The sessions were designed to be interactive and age-appropriate, inspiring simple, meaningful actions from a young age.

## Materials
- Earth Day Poster
- Exoplanet "Barcodes" video
