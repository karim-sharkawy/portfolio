# 11/22/63 — Stephen King

A time-travel thriller where a teacher attempts to prevent the assassination of John F. Kennedy. The novel explores the butterfly effect, the nature of time, and the cost of changing history.

## Themes
- Time travel and alternate history
- Moral dilemmas
- The weight of historical events

## Why I Read It
Beyond the entertainment value, this book made me think about causality and prediction, which parallels concepts in my machine learning work on forecasting and causal inference.
