# Workshop Facilitator at Climate Resilient Communities

I contributed as a speaker and workshop facilitator for youth-focused programming on climate data storytelling and citizen science.

## Activities
- Co-designed workshops combining discussion, activities, and local data
- Focused on making climate issues tangible and relevant to participants' lives
- Introduced accessible data collection and interpretation methods
- Used gamified approaches to encourage creativity and collaboration
- Centered narratives on marginalized communities

## Goals
To equip participants with practical skills for community engagement and data-informed climate action.

This work emphasized the bridge between technical tools and community-driven knowledge.
