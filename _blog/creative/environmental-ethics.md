# Environmental Ethics — Gregory Bassham

A comprehensive introduction to environmental philosophy, exploring different ethical frameworks for addressing environmental challenges. The book examines anthropocentric vs. ecocentric approaches to conservation and sustainability.

## Key Topics
- Ethical theories applied to environmental issues
- Biodiversity and species preservation
- Climate change ethics
- Sustainable development

## Why I Read It
This book deepened my understanding of the ethical dimensions of environmental science, complementing my atmospheric research and commitment to sustainability.
