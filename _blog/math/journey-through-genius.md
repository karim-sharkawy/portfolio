# Journey Through Genius — William Dunham

A captivating journey through 12 great theorems in mathematics, presented with historical context and clear explanations. Dunham masterfully combines mathematical rigor with storytelling.

## Key Theorems Covered
- Pythagorean Theorem
- Euler's Formula
- Gödel's Incompleteness Theorem
- And more...

## Why I Read It
This book inspired me to appreciate the historical development of mathematical ideas and their connections to my work in linear algebra and optimization.
