# ML Engineer (Volunteer) | Sprout Remote (04/2025–Present)

## Overview
As a volunteer ML Engineer at Sprout Remote, I focus on applying machine learning to support mental health initiatives, particularly in analyzing climate anxiety through social media data.

## Key Responsibilities and Achievements

- **NLP Pipeline Development**: Developed comprehensive NLP pipelines to analyze 270GB of text data for recognizing climate anxiety across 2 mental health organizations. This involved processing 21M social media posts using advanced transformer models to extract themes and emotions.

- **Efficiency Improvements**: Cut processing time from 2 weeks to 15 hours by automating Azure ML workflows, ensuring interpretable outputs for stakeholders.

- **Team Leadership**: Led a 5-person cross-functional Agile team to deliver production-ready NLP solutions aligned with organizational needs.

- **Stakeholder Communication**: Designed emotionally-aware presentations and reports to surface interpretable insights for non-technical audiences, including educators and counselors.

## Technologies Used
- Python
- Transformers (HuggingFace)
- Azure ML
- PySpark

## Impact
- Processed 270GB of text data in 15 hours (vs. 2 weeks)
- Analyzed 21M posts for mental health insights
- Enabled data-driven interventions for climate anxiety
