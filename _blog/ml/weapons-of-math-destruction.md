# Weapons of Math Destruction — Cathy O'Neil

A critical examination of how big data and mathematical models can perpetuate inequality and injustice. O'Neil exposes the dangers of opaque algorithms in areas like criminal justice, education, and employment.

## Key Themes
- The opacity of algorithmic decision-making
- How models can amplify existing biases
- The need for accountability in AI systems

## Why I Read It
This book reinforced my commitment to ethical ML practices and interpretable models, influencing my approach to building responsible AI systems.
