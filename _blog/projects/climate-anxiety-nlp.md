## Problem

Mental health organizations needed to understand climate anxiety from social media data. Required analyzing large volumes of text to extract themes and emotions that could inform interventions and educational programs.

## Solution

**NLP Analysis Pipeline:**

1. Developed pipelines to process 270GB text data from social media
2. Used transformers to extract themes and emotions from 21M posts
3. Automated Azure ML workflows for efficient processing
4. Ensured interpretable outputs for stakeholders
5. Led cross-functional team to align technical solutions with organizational needs

## Results

- Processed 270GB of text data in 15 hours (vs 2 weeks manual)
- Analyzed 21M posts for climate anxiety insights
- Delivered emotionally-aware reports to non-technical stakeholders
