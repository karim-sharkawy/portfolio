I build machine learning systems that solve real problems, from NLP pipelines analyzing climate anxiety to ensemble models forecasting weather patterns. I care about the full lifecycle: from data processing and model development to deployment and monitoring.

## My Approach

**Systems First** – I believe ML is 90% infrastructure and 10% algorithms. I optimize for maintainability, scalability, and observability.

**Pragmatic** – I ship solutions that solve real problems. Bias toward simple, understandable models that work over complex black boxes.

**Rigorous** – I care about experimental design, proper validation, and honest error analysis.

**Clear Communication** – I explain complex ideas in simple terms. Good documentation and communication are as important as good code.

## Background

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from Purdue University with a Data Science Certificate and Atmospheric Science Minor. My journey spans research in optimization and climate dynamics to applied ML engineering in NLP and forecasting.

I've worked on:
