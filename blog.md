## How I Blog

- **Quality over frequency** – I write when I have something worth sharing
- **Systems thinking** – I focus on lessons that apply to building, not just doing ML
- **Honest** – I share failures and lessons equally with wins
- **Clear** – I write for the interested reader, not the expert
